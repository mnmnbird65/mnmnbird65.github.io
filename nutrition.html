Daily Nutrition Essentials

Nutrients Purpose Healthy Sources
Carbohydrates Main source of energy Whole grains, fruits, vegetables
Proteins Build & repair body tissue Lean meats, beans, eggs, tofu, fish
Fats Supports brain function & vitamin absorption Olive oil, nuts, avocados
Vitamins & Minerals Boost immunity & help organs function Colorful fruits and vegetables
Water Maintains hydration & body function Aim for 2 litres per day

Nutrition by meal

Breakfast

Start your day with a mix of complex carbs, proteins, and some healthy fat to stay energized.

Examples:
  • brown rice bowl
  • whole-grain toast
  • natto
  • scrambled eggs
  • yogult
  • fruits
  • veggie soup

Lunch

Focus on balance portion to refuel your energy for the afternoon.

Examples
  • brown rice bowl
  • whole-grain toast
  • grilled chicken
  • vegetables
  • sishi

Dinner

Choose lighter but nutritious meals to support recovery and rest.

Examples
  • Baked salmon
  • quinoa salad
  • steamed broccoli
  • veggie soup

Snacks

Keep snack healthy and portion-controlled to maintain energy

  • Fruits
  • nuts
  • smoothie

Tips for Balanced Eating

  1. Eat at regular intervals - don't skip meals!!
  2. Fill half your plate with vegetables and fruit
  3. Limit processed foods and added sugars and artificial colorings
