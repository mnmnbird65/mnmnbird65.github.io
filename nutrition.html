Daily Nutrition Essentials

  • Carbohydrates: The main source of energy - choose whole grains, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Proteins: Build and repair body tissues - include lean meats, beans, eggs, tofu, or fish.
  • Fats: Support brain function and vitamin absorption - focus on healthy fats like olive oil, nuts, and avocados.
  • Vitamins & Minerals: Strengthen immunity and help organs function properly - eat a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables.
  • Water: Stay hydrated! Aim for 2 liters

Tips for Balanced Eating

  1. Eat at regular intervals - don't skip meals!!
  2. Fill half your plate with vegetables and fruit
  3. Limit processed foods and added sugars and artificial colorings
