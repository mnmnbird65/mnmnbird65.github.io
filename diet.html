1. Balanced Diet

the most recommended and flexible diet that includes food from all groups in moderate portions

  • Benefits: Easy to maintain, promotessteady energy and overall wellness
  • Sample Meal: Grilled chicken, brown rice, steamed broccoli

2. Mediterranean Diet

Inspired by the eating habits of countries bordering the Mediterranean sea, emphasizing grains, vegetables, olive oil, and nuts.

  • Benefits: Supports heart health, reduces inflammation, and improves longevity.
  • Sample Meal: Salmon with quinoa, mized greens with olive oil dressing, and almonds.

3. Vegetarian / Plant-Based Diet

This focuses on fruits, begetables, legumes, nuts, and grains, limiting or eliminating animal products.

  • Benefits: Lower cholesterol, improve digestion
  • Sample Meal: soup, vegetable stir-fry, and tofu fry

Note: Always consult a healthcare professional or nutritional before starting a new diet plan, especially if you have specific health concerns.
